Coffee in Melbourne

One persons coffee journey around the suburbs of Melbourne in search of the perfect drink.

June 2, 2015

My first day of the Odyssey! I can't believe I finally got every cafe in Melbourne mapped out and am now starting this amazing journey. I am equipped with the essentials: a mobile phone, digital camera, note pad and a bottle of water. As Lao Tu would have said, " A Journey of a thousand miles begins with one step."


July 14, 2015
